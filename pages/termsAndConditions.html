TERMS AND CONDITIONS


Introduction

Adventure Leisure Limited (ALL) takes the confidentiality, safety and security of our customers seriously. Please read the below terms and conditions before making a booking with Sidcup Family Golf/ALL. Should you have any questions regarding your data or our privacy policy please click here.


BOOKINGS

On completion of your booking, the system will automatically send an email confirmation. This must be shown on arrival. Should a confirmation email not be received, please contact the Sidcup Family Golf team at reception@sidcupfamilygolf.com to check your booking.


Payment

For all bookings full payment is required online by Debit or Credit Card at the time of booking. (Please note that American Express is not accepted.


Please note that all payments made online are non-refundable.


Range Cards

Range card credit has 1 year expiry, so if it’s not used within 12 months of loading it onto the card it will expire. We cannot refund range card credit once it has been loaded. Discount rates are subject to change.


Vouchers and Promotion Codes

You must enter any vouchers or promotional codes, and ensure the code has been accepted, and price adjusted, before entering your payment details. No credits or refunds can be made in the event your voucher / promotion code is not recognised online. In this event please contact your Sidcup Family Golf site before making payment.


Gift Vouchers

Any gift vouchers purchased can be claimed at the venue. All gift vouchers are valid for 12 months from date of purchase. Gift vouchers are redeemable against driving range and crazy golf.


On Arrival

All guests that have booked online must check in at reception during the allocated slot and collect the validated scorecard (if applicable) and receipt for the booking.


Cancellation

Cancellation should be made by telephone to Sidcup Family Golf.


Payment for cancellations made more than 24 hours’ notice will be transferred to a future booking. Cancellations with less than 24 hours’ notice or ‘no-shows’ are fully chargeable. Please note that all payments made online are non-refundable.

Photos & Videos

ALL reserves the right to refuse permission or to withdraw permission of anyone using any photographic recording equipment should the ALL’s Management feel that the safety and wellbeing of its customers has been or would be compromised.
